- `src/lib/supabase/schema.sql` - Phase 4.1 section appended with 6 UPDATE statements by product slug

## Decisions Made

- `price_tiers` is **required** (not optional) on `ProductVariant` — every variant must declare its tier array; no special-casing needed downstream
- `variant.price` is retained as the lowest-tier price, used for display and sort; the full tier array lives in `price_tiers`
- 24K Gold 2mm is the only bead with a 25-bead tier; all other 2mm/4mm start at qty 50; all 6mm variants start at qty 25
- Shears uses `[{qty:1,price:3500}]` — consistent `PriceTier[]` shape avoids null checks in cart logic
